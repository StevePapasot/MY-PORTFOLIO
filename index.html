Let's talk about me

I’m a Greek UX/UI designer and creative web developer with a passion for solving problems through simple & interactive designs.

I am an Electrical Engineer graduate, and curiosity dragged me to learn web development. Then, I learned UX/UI design, JavaScript and React js , among many other things. Currently, I’m exploring music and audio effects on websites.

I love typography & colours and consider myself a UI design generalist. Depending on the project requirement, I try to opt for a suitable design style—Minimalistic, colourful, typographic, tech, elegant, etc.—while sticking to the core design principles.

My development stack is focused on performance & performanceaccessibility with delightful interactions.
